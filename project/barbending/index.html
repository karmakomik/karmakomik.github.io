Rebar Bending Simulator

Oct 26, 2019

I led the software development team in the Reinforced bar bending project. I specifically created the procedural and mesh deformation graphics underlying the simulator as well as the virtual construction environment. The simulator is currently deployed in 5 Construction Skills Training Institutes of Larse & Toubro across India.
