About Me

I am a passionate dabbler in the intersection of humans, technology and education. I have been working in Amrita University's AMMACHI labs as a software engineer for the past 6 years exploring haptic technologies for skill training, computational thinking for school children, serious games for rehabilitation and social awareness among others. I have spent the past two years working with Indian rural communities (adopted by our university's parent NGO, Embracing the World) supporting village women build toilets in their communities and teaching village children basics of programming, electronics and robotics through games and theater. These days I work on a game called Haathi Mera Saathi (My Elephant Friend), a suite of board and computer/tablet games for teaching computational thinking skills with a public release expected in Summer 2017 across the Andhra Pradesh State and Amrita Vidyalayam school systems.
